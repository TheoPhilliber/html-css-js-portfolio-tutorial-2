Vorta Particle Abatement System

Purpose

A system designed to effectively remove particles from semiconductor EFEMs and wafer-handling equipment.

Description

The system utilizes a specialized Mobile Electrostatic Carrier (MESC), which fits within a standard FOUP. The MESC is charged to a high voltage using pogo pins, then transported to different stations in the EFEM. The stored voltage on the MESC attracts and captures airborne particles, reducing contamination.

Benefits

Enhancing semiconductor equipment cleanliness improves yield. By integrating into a standard FOUP, the system ensures broad compatibility with EFEMs without requiring modifications. Traditional cleaning methods require manual intervention, which is costly and impractical in vacuum environments. This system automates and streamlines the process.

My Contribution

As the lead engineer, I oversee all testing, analyze results, and implement iterative improvements. While an external consulting team initially designed the PCB, I have since taken full responsibility for revisions and reworks, refining the system for enhanced performance and reliability.
